Garlic Bread

Description:

Garlic bread is a simple, delicious side dish made with bread, butter, and garlic. Itâ€™s crispy on the outside, soft on the inside, and the perfect companion to pasta dishes or soups.

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette or loaf of Italian bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoons fresh parsley, choppes
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix the butter, garlic, parsley, and salt in a small bowl.
  3. Slice the bread and spread the garlic butter mixture on each piece
  4. Place on a baking sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes, until golden and crispy.
  5. Serve warm.
